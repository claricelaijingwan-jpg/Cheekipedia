Favourite Songs

Wave

By: Ateez

When summer rolls around, this song will be no. 1 choice. Although this song out summer 2019, the vibe and rhythm is still timeless to dance off. This song is about a group of young boys going on adventure on the summer. Although there are challenges and set backs, they still won't give up and continue their adventure. When it first came out, I feel like I'm at the beach and playing at the beach with my friends. Whenever I feel down, I would listen to this song to cheer my mood up.

Hope is the thing with Feather

By: Robin Chevy

Hope is something that we believe to achieve something that we want in our lives. This song is from a game called Honkai Star Rail, which is under company Hoyoverse. This song is played during the 3rd stage of final boss fight game. This sing send message to never give up on our dreams, although it is seems to impossible to achieve it. We, as humans, are fighting on injustice that happened in our life. Although we might think our voices are not enough, we still believe on our dream to keep going on to live and strife to be achieve our dream goals

The Phoenix

By: Fall Out Boy

Phoenix is a myth creature that to believed to be able rebirth again and again. It is symbolize rebirth, renewal, hope, and transformation in many beliefs. In this song, it tell us how we should risen up after we failed or fallen down. In our lives, we do faces challenges and those challenges make us wanna to give up to continue our own "war". If the world make us backing down, we should change our strategy so that we can continue to "play" the game so that we can win our own game.
